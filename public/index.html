About Our Club

The BIS Standards Club at VIT Vellore is dedicated to promoting awareness and understanding of quality standards in engineering and technology. Our mission is to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and industry requirements through workshops, seminars, and hands-on projects.

Founded in 2020, our club collaborates with the Bureau of Indian Standards to provide students with opportunities to engage with standardization processes and quality management practices that are essential in today's competitive job market.
